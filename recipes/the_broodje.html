The Broodje

Description


To make a ham and truffle sandwich, begin by toasting two slices of your favorite bread to add texture. Once toasted, spread a generous amount of Kewpie mayo on one side of each slice, infusing a rich, umami flavor. Lay ham slices on one piece of bread, adjusting the quantity to your liking. Add truffle cheese, either in slices or grated, atop the ham to introduce a luxurious, aromatic taste. Close the sandwich with the other slice of bread, mayo side facing inwards. Optionally, you can briefly grill the sandwich to melt the cheese and meld the flavors. Once ready, serve the sandwich immediately, enjoying the gourmet blend of ham and truffle cheese in this simple yet elegant meal.


Ingredients


  • bread
  • Truffle cheese
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • York ham

Steps

  1. Slices of your favorite bread (2 slices per sandwich) Kewpie mayo Truffle cheese (sliced or grated) Ham slices
  2. Prepare the Bread: Start by toasting the bread slices to your preferred level of crispiness. This adds texture and flavor to the sandwich.
  3. Once the bread is toasted, spread a generous amount of Kewpie mayo on one side of each slice. The unique taste of Kewpie mayo adds a rich, umami flavor.
  4. Place ham slices on one piece of bread, covering the mayo. The amount of ham can vary depending on your preference.
  5. Add slices or sprinkle grated truffle cheese over the ham. The truffle cheese is the star ingredient, providing a luxurious and aromatic flavor.
  6. Close the sandwich with the other slice of bread, mayo side down.
  7. Optional: You can lightly grill the sandwich in a pan to melt the cheese and bring all the flavors together.
